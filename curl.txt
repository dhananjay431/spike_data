Create an AI-powered Real-Time Market Spike Detection Dashboard.

Objective:
Build a professional dashboard that continuously monitors live stock market JSON data and instantly detects sudden spikes, unusual movements, high volume activity, breakout patterns, and abnormal price changes.

Data Format:
Use market data JSON structure similar to:

* symbol
* open
* dayHigh
* dayLow
* lastPrice
* previousClose
* change
* pChange
* totalTradedVolume
* totalTradedValue
* yearHigh
* yearLow
* timestamp
* companyName
* industry

Example:

* RELIANCE
* INFY
* SBIN
* HDFCBANK
* TCS
* ADANIENT
